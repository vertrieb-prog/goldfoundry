# GOLD FOUNDRY — MEMORY.md

## Brand Voice
Premium but accessible. Confident, not arrogant. Data-driven, not hype.
Think: Bloomberg meets modern fintech. Gold + Black. Minimalist luxury.
German as primary language, English for international. No emojis in code.

## Target Audience
1. Prop Firm Traders (80%): FTMO, FundedNext, Tag Markets. Need risk management.
2. Retail Forex Traders: Want copy trading without learning to trade.
3. Crypto Traders: Binance/Bybit/Bitget users who want better risk tools.
4. Network Marketing Leaders: Want deep MLM with unlimited depth.
5. Complete Beginners: "Ich will Geld verdienen ohne Trading zu lernen."

## What We Learned
- "AI" in product names scares regulators → use "Smart", "Intelligent", "FORGE"
- "GRATIS" attracts freeloaders → "80% Rabatt" attracts buyers
- Dollar prices confuse EU audience → always Euro
- Props firms are THE market → every feature connects to Challenge Tracker
- Leaders want DEPTH not width → unlimited level MLM
- Share Cards with USER PROFIT (not trader performance) convert 3x better
- First month 80% rabatt → commission calculated on RABATT price

## Content Rules

- No "guaranteed", "risk-free", "100% safe"
