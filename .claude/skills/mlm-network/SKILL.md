8 hidden bonuses: Generational, Power Start, Retention, Re-Activation, First-to-Rank, Anniversary, Rollup, Contests.
7 ranks: Starter→Bronze→Silber→Gold→Platin→Diamond→Crown.

KYC: only at payout (ID front+back+selfie). Not at signup.
Payout: min 5000 FP (€500). USDT/BTC/SEPA. Auto-approve rules.
