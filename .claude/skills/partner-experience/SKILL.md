# Partner Experience
AI Partner Coach: personalized advice, message templates, network analysis, content generation. Uses Haiku.
Fast Start Plan: 4-week plan based on goal (Nebenverdienst/Business/Vollzeit/Leader).
Share Cards: auto-generated daily/weekly/monthly. USER PROFIT not trader performance. Partner ref-link IN image.
Share Buttons: on EVERY page. WhatsApp/Telegram/Insta/Twitter/Email/QR. Auto ref-parameter.
Trade Screenshots: auto-generated, branded, with ref-link. Partner sees "3 new screenshots available".
Content Generator: Trade Result/Lifestyle/Education/Partner-Pitch × Platform.
Milestones: 13+ milestones with FP rewards + shareable badges.
Hot Leads: track visitors who visited partner page without signup. Score them.
Daily Tasks: 5 tasks/day, gamified, streak system (14d=200FP, 30d=500FP).
Training Academy: 4 levels (Grundlagen/Verkaufen/Team/Leadership), videos+quizzes, certificates.
Reports: daily/weekly/monthly, configurable per partner. Email + Push.
"Verpasste Boni" widget: shows what partner COULD have earned.
"Empfohlen von" banner: global, cookie-based (90 days), on every page.
Upline Support: sponsor contact, shared materials, team-call calendar.
A/B Testing: automatic LP optimization. Partner does nothing.
Community Feed: posts, likes, tips, announcements.
Income Predictor: 3 scenarios (conservative/realistic/optimistic) over 12 months.
Trader LPs per partner: /trader/[slug]?ref=partner or /r/[partner]/[trader].
